WHAT DO YOU THINK?

Agata Popek

11.10.2024

W starym Kadrze

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

This spot has such an incredible vibe—it’s the perfect blend of cozy and fun! The coffee is top-notch, brewed to perfection with a variety of options to please any palate. The seating is super comfortable, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere that’s ideal for relaxing or catching up with friends. Plus, there’s a mini cinema right on site, making it a unique destination for a chill hangout or an unforgettable movie date. The snacks are just as delightful, with a great selection of tasty treats to pair with your drink or enjoy during the film. Whether you’re looking for a quiet corner to unwind or a fun space to make memories, this place truly has it all!

W starym Kadrze

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Checked out this cute little arthouse cinema café the other day. Love the vibe—super artsy and cozy, and they play the coolest indie movies. BUT... the food and drinks were kinda overpriced, and service was slow (maybe they were understaffed?). Still a cool spot to hang, just don’t go hungry!

W starym Kadrze

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Had a great time at W Starym Kadrze! The combination of a cozy cafe and cinema is perfect for a relaxed night out. The coffee was delicious, and the menu had plenty of tasty options. The atmosphere was warm and inviting, with comfy seating and a great selection of films. My only complaint was that the sound quality in the cinema room could’ve been a bit better, but overall, it was a wonderful experience. I’ll definitely be back!
